# Protos

Protos is an educational Flutter application designed to help users understand protein, amino acids, and overall nutrition through science-backed content and a comprehensive food nutrition database.

The app focuses on building strong fundamentals in nutrition science while providing practical tools to support healthier dietary choices.

---

## About the App

Protos is built to make nutrition understandable, accurate, and actionable.  
It explains why nutrients matter, not just how much to consume.

Key objectives:
- Strengthen core concepts in nutrition science
- Provide reliable and diverse food composition data
- Enable users to track and improve dietary habits

## Educational Content

The app includes structured educational material covering:

### Protein Basics
- What protein is
- Why protein is essential for the body
- How protein supports growth, repair, and metabolism

### Amino Acids Guide
- Essential vs non-essential amino acids
- Complete and incomplete protein sources
- Biological roles of amino acids

### Nutrition Science
- Macronutrients: carbohydrates, fats, and proteins
- Micronutrients: vitamins and minerals
- Principles of balanced diets

### Indian Ingredients
- Detailed nutrition data for commonly used Indian ingredients
- Regionally relevant food information

---

## Features

### Search Database
- Search thousands of food items
- View protein, carbohydrates, fats, vitamins, and minerals

### Nutrition Calculator
- Log meals
- Track protein and nutrient intake over time

## Food Categories

The database is organized into the following categories:

- Baked Products
- Sweets and Snacks
- Vegetables and Vegetable Products
- Others
- Fats and Oils
- Poultry and Dairy Products
- Meat and Meat Products
- Fishes and Seafoods
- Fruits and Fruit Products
- Pulses, Nuts and Oilseeds
- Cereals and Grain Products
- Beverages
- Condiments and Sauces

---

## Data Sources

Nutrition data is compiled from multiple international and national databases:

- USDA (SR Legacy)
- Frida Database (Denmark)
- Standard Food Composition Table (Korea)
- Indian Diet Data Portal
- Indian Food Composition Table
- Canadian Nutrient File
- FAO/INFOODS
- Kenyan Food Composition Table
